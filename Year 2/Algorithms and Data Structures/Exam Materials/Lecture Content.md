# Lecture 1

Types of time complexity

**Good**
Constant: O(1)
Logarithmic: O(log n)
Linear: O(n)

**Acceptable (Sometimes)**
Quadratic: O(n<sup>2</sup>)
Cubic: O(n<sup>3</sup>)
Polynomial: O(n<sup>k</sup>)

**Intractable**
Exponential: O(a<sup>n</sup>)
Factorial O(n!)
